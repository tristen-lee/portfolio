Where I'm Headed

I am still figuring it out, and I think that's okay. Right now I am exploring different programming languages to find the ones that click for me. What I know for sure is that I genuinely love building apps. It is fun, it is creative, and every time I learn something new I want to keep going.

Data and analytics have been a big pull for me. Working with data in my Python mood tracker opened my eyes to how powerful it can be. Visualizing patterns, finding meaning in numbers. I want to go deeper there.

I also have a background in video game development and want to revisit it. Indie dev is a grind, but the idea of building something people actually play still excites me.

Whatever I build, I want it to be accessible and up to standard, usable by everyone. That matters to me personally and I plan to carry it into everything I make.
